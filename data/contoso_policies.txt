# Contoso Policies

## Return Policy

### General Guidelines
- All products may be returned within 30 days of purchase
- Products must be in original condition with all packaging
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

### Exceptions
- Custom-configured hardware: 14-day return window
- Software licenses: Non-refundable once activated
- Professional services: Non-refundable

### Process
1. Contact support at returns@contoso.com
2. Receive Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
3. Ship product with RMA number clearly marked
4. Refund issued upon inspection

## Privacy Policy

### Data Collection
Contoso collects only necessary information to provide services:
- Contact information (name, email, phone)
- Billing information (processed securely)
- Usage data (anonymized for product improvement)

### Data Protection
- All data encrypted at rest and in transit
- GDPR and CCPA compliant
- Regular security audits conducted
- Data retained only as long as necessary

### Your Rights
- Request access to your data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Data portability upon request

## Support Policy

### Response Times
- Critical issues: 1 hour response
- High priority: 4 hour response
- Normal priority: 8 hour response
- Low priority: 24 hour response

### Escalation Path
1. Initial support ticket
2. Senior support engineer
3. Technical account manager
4. Executive escalation

Contact: support@contoso.com or 1-800-CONTOSO
